    • Research

    I'm interested in devleoping combinatorial optimization models, particularly the submodular optimization model, for power system reliability, security and risk mitigation. I'm also engaged in utilizing complex network theory to analyze power system, especially the evolution pattern of cascading failure. In short, I'm attempting to investigate the power system reliability & security problems from the perspectives of operation research, computer science and mathematics theory.
